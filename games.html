[ HOW_TO_PLAY ]

rule_01 — write a word. any word.

rule_02 — your next word must begin with the last letter of the previous one.

rule_03 — no two words may begin with the same letter.

goal — use as much of the alphabet as you can before you run out of moves.

// scoring

Score = (t / n)L

t = 26 · n = letters remaining · L = word length
longer words, later in the run, score exponentially more.
